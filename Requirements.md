### 1. Problem Definition

* **Target Community:**  Underserved populations with limited digital literacy or language barriers.
  
* **Gap:**  Lack of easy access to vital government schemes, healthcare information, or legal resources due to complex jargon and non-inclusive interfaces.

### 2. Functional Requirements (FR)

* **FR1: Multilingual Voice Interface:**  Users must be able to query the system using voice in at least two regional languages (e.g., Hindi and Marathi).

* **FR2: Automated Eligibility Engine:**  The system shall match user profiles (age, income, occupation) with relevant public resources or schemes.
  
* **FR3: Document Simplification:**  The AI shall summarize dense official notifications into simplified "Action Items."
  
* **FR4: Low-Bandwidth Mode:**  A text-only or USSD-compatible output for users in areas with poor internet connectivity.

### 3. Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

* **NFR1: Inclusivity:**  The interface must support high-contrast modes and screen readers (WCAG 2.1 compliance).
  
* **NFR2: Reliability:**  99.9% uptime is required as the tool provides critical public information.
  
* **NFR3: Privacy:**  No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) shall be stored beyond the session to ensure community trust.

### 4. Hardware/Software Constraints

* Must be accessible via low-cost Android devices.
  
* Must function efficiently on 3G/LTE networks.
